Applications of Quantum Computers
Quantum computers have the potential to revolutionize many fields due to their unique properties. They are particularly promising in areas such as cryptography,
where they could break certain encryption schemes that classical computers cannot efficiently solve. They are also useful in 
optimization problems, drug discovery, and material science, because they can simulate quantum systems naturally, 
something classical computers struggle to do. In machine learning, quantum algorithms could enable faster 
training of complex models, opening new possibilities in AI.
